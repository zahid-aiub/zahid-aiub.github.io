About My Self

I enrolled myself in Computer Science & Engineering at American International University - Bangladesh. I had completed my bachelor studies in Fall 2019. During the course of bachelor studies, I got introduced to various terms of computer science which inspired me to obtain more knowledge in the field of computer science so I desire to pursue my higher education in the area of Computer Science.


In my academic project I developed a real life vehicle tracking system, where I worked as a lead. The tracking device worked over the network and the device has some controls over the vehicle engines. The device is controlled through an android application. From that project I learned lots of tech things and got appreciation from my project supervisors which make me today more enthusiastic to gather more knowledge in this field. From my bachelor studies, I have obtained knowledge on Computer Programming, Data structure & Algorithms, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Software development life-cycle & process models and many other staffs that help me to solve real life problems with the help of technology. The strength of my academics is Computer programming and Algorithms. This discipline and eagerness help me to think smartly and solve problems efficiently.
